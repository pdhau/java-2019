Company A require build system to calculate salary of employee by position.

We have 3 positions:
+ Manager
+ Leader
+ Staff

With position is manager : salary = revenue * 10%
With position is Leader : salary = basic salary * pay rate + benifit (pay rate = 4.2 , benifit = 300$)
With position is Staff: salary = basic salary * pay rate (pay rate = 3.9)

If employee is female will be add 50$ to salary each month
